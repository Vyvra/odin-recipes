Pasta

Description

One of my childrens favorite dishes is this pasta dish, it has everything that a growing child would need and is vegetarian to boost.

If you like vegetables as much as I do this recipe will surely make you smile.

Ingredients

  • Dry Macaroni
  • One Onion
  • Several cloves of garlic
  • One Courgette
  • Two bell peppers
  • One pack or vegetarian minced meat
